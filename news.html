Stay informed with the latest developments in the WSBSD project.

May 14th 2025

WSBSD 1.0.0.4-5 Is Delayed

We regret to inform you that the release of WSBSD 1.0.0.4-5 has been delayed. We have a busy summer ahead of us, the estimated release date is now August 31st 2025. Sorry the delay will be long. We Are now pausing the WSBSD Project and 1.0.0.4-5 brainstorming.

June 4th 2025

WSBSD 1.0.0.4-4 Releases

WSBSD 1.0.0.4-4 is now available for download. This release includes various bug fixes and performance improvements.

Here's The Full changelog At GitHub: https://github.com/TTSConsulting/WSBSD/compare/1.0.0.4-3...1.0.0.4-4

June 17th 2025

WSBSD 1.0.0.4-5 Delay Stops

We are pleased to announce that the delay for WSBSD 1.0.0.4-5 has been lifted. Development is back on track, and we are working hard to meet the new release date. Looks Like Our Busy summer is shorter then expected!

July 31st 2025

WSBSD 1.0.0.4-5 Releases

We are excited to announce that WSBSD 1.0.0.4-5 has been released! This version includes several new features and improvements. Thank you for your patience during the development process.

The Reason Why it took so long is of busy summer and laziness
